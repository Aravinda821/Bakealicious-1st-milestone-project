About US

Bakealicious had its humble beginning in 2022, when I baked brownies for an office party. The response was overwhelming, which sparked the idea of turning it into a side hustle.

I grew up in a family where my mother’s and grandmother’s cooking skills were legendary. Guests were a constant presence at our home, which meant it was always hustle time in the kitchen. My mother and her three sisters are all exceptional cooks, each with their own specialty. My mother's forte, however, was baking—rarely did a week go by without a fresh cake in the house.

From the start, Bakealicious focused on what we do best: crafting products from scratch using traditional baking methods and wholesome ingredients. We believe that taste should always come first, with presentation being secondary.

This philosophy—honouring traditional heirloom recipes and doing them right—has served us well. We remain committed to our original values: handcrafting every product with the freshest and finest ingredients, using local produce whenever possible, avoiding preservatives, and baking everything to order to ensure quality and freshness.

We're proud that some of our team members have been with us since the very beginning. At Bakealicious, we believe that every product should be baked not only with premium ingredients but also with love.

As we continue this journey, we thank each and every one of you who has walked through our doors and taken home handcrafted cakes and sweets—from our home to yours.

With love and gratitude,
The Bakealicious Family
